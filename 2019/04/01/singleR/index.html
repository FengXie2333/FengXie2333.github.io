    Reference-based analysis of lung single-cell sequencing reveals a transitional profibrotic macrophage

    Results

    Reference data sets enhance annotation of scRNA-seq

    Pipeline

    The SingleR pipeline is based on correlating reference bulk transcriptomic data sets of pure cell types with single-cell gene expression.In the first step of the SingleR pipeline, a Spearman coefficient was calculated for single-cell gene expression with each of the samples in the reference data set, using only the variable genes in the reference data set. Using only variable genes among the cell types increased the ability to distinguish closely related cell types. This process was performed iteratively using only the top cell types from the previous step and the variable genes among them until only one cell type remaine

    Validate SingleR

    Using SingleR to analyze a published study of scRNA-seq of mouse bone marrow-derived dendritic cells (BMDC) and fibroblasts. When applied to the ImmGen database as a reference library, SingleR identified 33 of the 48 cells thought in the study to be BMDCs as macrophages

    原文中用GM-CSF培养细胞，然后sorting CD11c+ 的细胞。这个protocol已被证明无法获得纯的DC细胞，会混进表达marcrophage markers的细胞。

    SingleR identifies a transitional macrophage in fibrosis

    applied SingleR to enhance annotation of a lung scRNA-seq data set obtained from whole lungs of adult, male mice (10–12 weeks age) dissociated into single-cell suspension and flow sorted on live cells, using 6 steady-state mice and 3 mice 14 days after a single tracheal instillation of bleomycin, the fibrotic phase of the injury model.

    conclusion: The number of lung macrophages was higher in the bleomycin-treated condition than at steady state

    Macrophages and dendritic cells have similar transcriptomic profiles. So the authors repeated SingleR analysis using published RNA-seq data sets of mouse lung macrophages and dendritic cells to isolate the macrophages in the pooled bleomycin-injured and steady-state samples. Finally, singleR identified two subtypes of macrophages: alveolar and interstitial macrophages

    Similarity analysis by comparison of each cell to mouse RNA-seq data sets of alveolar and interstitial macrophages indicated a gradient of cellular identity in the macrophage cluster between alveolar and interstitial macrophages

    Deconvolution analysis in was performed using the DeconRNA-seq package using the average expression of AM samples and average expression of IM3 samples from GSE94135

    Then, the full range of SingleR correlation scores for each cell with reference to ImmGen was used for unsupervised hierarchical clustering of the macrophages from the lung scRNA-seq data set. This analysis identified three distinct subclusters (C1, C2, and C3), all three detected at varying levels in both steady-state and bleomycin-treated mice.45% of cluster C2 cells (but only 7.3% of cluster C1 cells and 4.7% of cluster C3 cells) expressed at least 33% of cluster C1 genes and at least 33% of cluster C3 genes, suggesting that cluster C2 was intermediate in gene expression between C1 and C3

    conclusion:cluster C1 was equally present in steady-state lung and bleomycin injury and corresponded to the cells that were annotated as alveolar macrophages by the lung-specific data sets; clusters C2 and C3 were enriched in cells isolated from mice with bleomycin-induced fibrosis and expressed Cx3cr1, Ccr2, Mafb, and MHCII genes, consistent with monocytic origin

    Clustering in Fig. 3a was performed using Ward’s hierarchical agglomerative clustering method based on the SingleR scores across all cell types

    Cluster C2 and C3 markers are found in alternate lung fibrosis models

    To test the relevance of the identified gene expression pat-tern to other models of fibrosis, the authors used a mouse model of lung fibrosis induced by epithelial telomere dysfunction

    用human芯片数据验证，发现在小鼠中得出的结论也使用与人.immunofluorescence of lung tissue indicated the expression of MAFB, a marker detected specifically in clusters C2 and C3, in CD68+ macrophages from patients with idiopathic lung fibrosis compared with healthy controls(Figc)

    Cluster C2 macrophages localize to the fibrotic niche

    Conclusion: macrophage cluster C2 localizes to the fibrotic niche

    CX3CR1-expressing macrophages drive lung fibrosis

    Conclusion：during fibrogenesis, CX3CR1+ SiglecF+ macrophages localize to sites of Pdgfra+ and Pdgfrb+ fibroblast accumulation after bleomycin-induced injury in the lung. Deletion of these macrophages during the fibrotic period (days 8–21) eliminated Pdgfra+ or Pdgfrb+ fibroblasts and decreased total lung collagen. Mechanistic studies indicated that fibroblast migration and proliferation were dependent on secretion of Pdgf-aa by SiglecF+ CD11c+ MHCIIhi and CX3CR1+ cells, respectively, suggesting that paracrine interactions between these macrophages and fibroblasts sustain the fibroblast compartment and tissue fibrosis.
