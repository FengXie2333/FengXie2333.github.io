Targeting CXCL12 from FAP-expressing carcinoma-associated fibroblasts synergizes with anti–PD-L1 immunotherapy in pancreatic cancer

PNAS, 2013

在胰腺癌中，α-CTLA-4 and α-PD-L1治疗效果不佳。

ELISpots 显示， 来自肿瘤的CD8 T细胞中 IFN-γ–secreting CD8+ T cells 更多。而且The frequency of IFN-γ–secreting CD8+ T cells was the same when the stimulating tumor cells were from the T-cell donor or from another PDA-bearing mouse

Conclusion：PDA荷瘤小鼠对于肿瘤抗原有自发适应性免疫。免疫抑制剂疗效不佳，有别的原因

FAP+ cells were present in PanIN and both cytokeratin-19+ (CK19+) and CK19− PDA lesions but rarely α-smooth muscle actin (αSMA), whereas FAP+ cells among PDA cells were CD34−and αSMA+

All FAP+ cells were PDGF receptor-α+ and CD45−, confirming their mesenchymal origin

RNAseq 显示胰腺癌中FAP+细胞编码基因与细胞外基质蛋白相关

Depleting FAP+ cells slowed PDA growth, but not when CD4+ and CD8+ T cells were removed. Combining depletion of FAP+ cells with administration of α-CTLA-4 or α-PD-L1 further diminished tumor growth.

Conclusion：FAP+ 细胞介导PDA对于免疫抑制剂的抵抗作用

DTx- and α-PD-L1–treated mice 中IFN-γ–secreting CD8+ T cells并没有增加。

Conclusion：FAP的作用并不是通过诱导CD8细胞

癌旁缺乏CD3+ T细胞，原因是因为CXCL12。作者发现肿瘤中FAP+ 细胞是CXCL12的唯一来源。但是CXCL12定位在肿瘤细胞上，可能是metabolically stressed cancer cells过表达并分泌HMGB1捕获CXCL12并形成高亲和力的复合物。

使用CXCR4抑制剂 AMD3100， 证实了CXCL12的作用。Tumor growth was slowed by AMD3100 in a T cell-dependent manner。 CXCR4-mediated exclusion of T cells reflects T-cell apoptosis
